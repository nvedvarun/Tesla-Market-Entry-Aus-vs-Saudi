## Key Finding
**Recommendation:** Enter **Australia** first.

Australia showed:
- Stronger EV adoption momentum  
- More mature charging & regulatory environment  
- Lower execution risk and faster path to profitability  

Saudi Arabia presents long-term potential but requires larger upfront investment and offers slower initial demand.
